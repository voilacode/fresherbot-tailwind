About Us

The Launchpad program is a focused and intensive training initiative designed to prepare students for success in job placements within a short span of one week. During this time, participants are provided with comprehensive training that covers essential technical skills, soft skills, and interview strategies, ensuring they are well-equipped to face competitive job markets.

The program emphasizes hands-on learning, mock interviews, and real-world problem-solving exercises, allowing students to build confidence and readiness. After completing the week-long training, students are sent to three job drives, where they apply the skills and knowledge gained from the program to secure their future careers.

This approach offers a structured pathway to employment, empowering students to step into their professional lives with a strong foundation and the ability to adapt to various workplace challenges.
