What we offer ?

DEDICATED CAMPUS DRIVE

A dedicated campus drive typically refers to a recruitment event by a company to hire students from a particular educational institution, such as a college or university. These events are designed to connect employers with potential candidates who are about to enter the job market .

WEEKLY SKILL TEST

Weekly skill tests can be beneficial in various contexts, from classroom education and personal development. They help ensure that learning and skill development are ongoing and that individuals are continually working to enhance their abilities in a structured and measurable way.

COMPANY MOCK ASSEMENTS

The primary purpose of mock assessments is to help candidates prepare for the real assessments used by the company. They serve as a tool for candidates to familiarize themselves with the format, type of questions, and expectations of the actual assessment.

PERFOMANCE REPORT

Information about student learning can be assessed through both direct and indirect measures. Direct measures may include exams, reports, projects, case study analysis, and other performances.
